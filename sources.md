# Sources

> `sources.md` — every source used, grouped and labelled by type. Vendor/commercial sources
> are marked **(V)** and their figures treated as upper-bound/directional. Consulted June 2026.

## A · Official statistics & institutions
- **Eurostat** — DESI digital-skills & enterprise AI-adoption indicators (EU hospitality AI
  deployment ~6% in 2023; digital-skills gap). 2023–25.
- **OECD** — enterprise AI-use surveys (accommodation/food vs finance/IT). 2025.
- **STATEC (Luxembourg)** — *Tourisme en chiffres 2024*; accommodation capacity 2023
  (~211–216 hotels, ~7,800 rooms, 1.4 M arrivals). statistiques.public.lu
- **European Commission** — *eInvoicing in Luxembourg* (Peppol volumes <100→~1.4 M).
  ec.europa.eu/digital-building-blocks
- **EU Council / EDICOM** — ViDA "VAT in the Digital Age" package (2025; intra-EU B2B 2030).

## B · Industry bodies & independent benchmarking
- **HOTREC** — *Skills and Labour Shortages in Hospitality: A Roadmap for Action* (~10% gap;
  ~10–11 M EU jobs). 2026. hotrec.eu
- **HotStats via HOTELSMag** — *Out of Recovery: Benchmarking European Hotels* (EU GOP ~36.5%;
  Western Europe 33.3%). 2026.
- **HVS** — *Hotel Profitability in Transition*. 2025.
- **CBRE via CRE Daily** — *Operating Costs 2025 Trends*. 2025.
- **The Hotel Blueprint** — EU workforce & AI-adoption/exposure analyses. 2025.

## C · Regulatory
- **KPMG Luxembourg** — mandatory e-invoicing requirements (LU law expected 2026; B2B 2028–29).
- **VATupdate** — e-invoicing & e-reporting in Luxembourg. 2026.
- **Novutech / Marosa VAT** — EU e-invoicing mandate guides (Belgium 2026; France 2026–27;
  Germany receive since 2025). 2025–26.

## D · AP automation benchmarks (mostly vendor — directional)
- **DocuClipper (V)** — 59 Accounts Payable Statistics for 2025 (manual ~68%; cycle 14.6 d).
- **HighRadius (V)**; **Quadient (V)**; **Nanonets (V)** — cost per invoice manual vs automated.
- **Artsyl (V)** — handling time 15→3 min. **Ascend (V)** — cycle times. **Grand View** — AP
  automation market ~12.8% CAGR.

## E · AI in hospitality (mixed — vendor/forecast)
- **Inn-Flow (V)** — AI in the Hotel Back Office survey (invoice processing top back-office want;
  "second set of eyes"). 2026.
- **h2c / Profitroom (V)** — global hotel AI study (8% with formal strategy; trust 6.6 vs
  reliance 4.7). 2025.
- **EHL Research** — AI in Hospitality (market $10.3B→$110.6B). 2025. **Sendbird (V)**;
  **Goldrich / Medium** (back-office 64% vs guest 9%). 2025–26.

## F · Public datasets (analogs / enrichment)
- **Hotel Booking Demand** — Antonio, Almeida & Nunes, *Data in Brief* 22 (2019); Kaggle
  (`jessemostipak/hotel-booking-demand`). Operational analog.
- **IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition** — Kaggle. Talent-pillar analog (synthetic, US).

> Operational dashboard data in this repo is **synthetic** (generated, labelled); market-evidence
> data is **real & public**, compiled into `data/processed/ai_adoption_evidence.csv`.
