# Timeline Estimate

> `cost_estimation/timeline_estimate.md` — indicative schedule. The pilot is the committed
> step; later phases depend on the decision gate.

| Phase | Duration | Key milestones |
|---|---|---|
| **0 · Preparation** | 1–2 weeks | Real volumes confirmed · DPA + EU processing · vendor master & COA loaded · confidence threshold set |
| **1 · Pilot** | 10 weeks | Flow live · weekly accuracy backtest · team trained · 4 success metrics tracked |
| 🚦 **Decision gate** | end of wk 10 | Touchless ≥ 65% & accuracy ≥ 90% at real volume → go |
| **2 · Rollout & hardening** | 8–10 weeks | All G&A vendors · monitoring · DPIA + AI-Act file · Peppol/e-invoice channel |
| **3 · Extend (roadmap)** | later | B: cost-control + stock · C: automated P&L reporting |

```
Wk:  0────2────────────────12 (gate)────────────22 ───────►  roadmap
     │Prep │      Pilot      │     Rollout/harden │   Extend (B, C)
```

**Total to a scaled, hardened solution:** ~5–6 months from kickoff, with a real go/no-go
decision at week ~12 costing only the ~€20k pilot to reach. Sequenced to land the e-invoicing
capability well ahead of the Luxembourg domestic B2B obligation (2028–29).
